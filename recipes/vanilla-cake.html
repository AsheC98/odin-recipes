Vanilla Cake

A delightfully light and easy Vanilla Cake recipe, perfect after a full savoury course.

Whilst the core of this cake recipe is vital, feel free to replace the toppings and cream as per your personal preference!

Ingredients

  • Baking paper
  • 350g flour
  • 120g white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  • 3 eggs
  • 100ml of milk

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, vanilla extract, and salt together in a large bowl. Add eggs and milk; mix by hand or beat with an electric mixer on low speed until smooth. Pour into the prepared pan.
  3. Bake until the centre of the cake is spongey. Place a cooling rack on top of the baking tray and tip upside down to remove the cake and allow it to cool.
  4. Once completely cooled, decorate the cake as desired!
