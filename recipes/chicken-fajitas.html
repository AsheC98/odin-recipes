Chicken Fajitas

A hearty and shareable meal of Chicken Fajitas, good for parties and families.

This simple recipe of Chicken Fajitas is bound to be a hit at your next social gathering!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon chilli powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper and salt
  • 4 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 4 boneless chicken breasts
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 1 onion
  • As many tortillas as required

Steps

  1. Whisk chili powder, paprika, salt, pepper, cayenne pepper, and garlic powder for fajita seasoning together in a small bowl.
  2. Add 2 tablespoons of olive oil and 3/4 of the fajita seasoning to the chicken breasts. Toss in a closed container until fully coated, then leave in the fridge to marinate.
  3. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees C and prepare the tortillas.
  4. Heat a pan of the remaining olive oil, then add peppers and onion. Stir in the remaining fajita seasoning and cook until vegetables are soft (8-10 minutes).
  5. Place the chicken in the oven and cook for 40 minutes. Meanwhile, prepare a station with the tortillas and vegetables for fajita assembly.
  6. Finally, once everything is cooked and ready, assemble fajitas - fill warmed tortillas with chicken, peppers, and onions.
