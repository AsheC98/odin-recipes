Chicken Fajitas

A hearty and shareable meal of Chicken Fajitas, good for parties and families.

This simple recipe of Chicken Fajitas is bound to be a hit at your next social gathering!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon chilli powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper and salt
  • 4 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 4 boneless chicken breasts
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 1 onion
  • As many tortillas as required
